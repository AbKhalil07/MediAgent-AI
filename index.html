Process

How MediAgent works

Six AI agents collaborate in milliseconds to deliver accurate, safe health guidance.

01
🛡️
Safety screening
Input is scanned for emergencies, PII is removed, crisis situations are immediately flagged with appropriate resources.
02
🚦
Triage classification
The Triage Agent classifies severity as Emergency, Urgent, or Routine and determines the appropriate care pathway.
03
🔬
Symptom analysis
The Symptom Agent extracts symptoms, generates differential diagnoses, assigns ICD-10 codes, and scores clinical risk.
04
💊
Drug safety check
The Drug Agent checks medication interactions and provides dosing guidance based on your symptoms and conditions.
05
📋
Report synthesis
The Report Agent compiles everything into a clear, empathetic, patient-friendly response with actionable next steps.
06
🧠
Continuous memory
Session state is maintained across turns — your symptoms, medications, and history are remembered throughout.
